Three Doors Down Bio

3 Doors Down formed in 1996 in Escatawpa, Mississippi. They rose to international fame with their first signle "Kyrptonite". Due to the success from Kyrptonite 3 Doors Down would go on to sign with Republic Record. They then relase their debut album The Better Life. This album would become the 11th best selling album of the year and be certified 6x platimum in the USA. 3 Doors Down sucuess continues with the bands Second Album Away from the Sun relased in 2002. This would icluded two of the bands biggest hits "When I'm Gone" and "Here Without You". The band would the go on tour for the next 2 years.
